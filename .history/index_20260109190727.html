Welcome

AgroSense and Water Innovation Lab

We are a group of researchers focusing on tackling problems on earth observation and geospatial analysis, specifically land use classification, change detection, environmental monitoring, crop yield prediction, and deep learning for satellite imagery analysis.

Ongoing Projects

Automated Change Detection

Detecting urban development and infrastructure changes using Sentinel-2 and SAR imagery.

Climate-Driven LULC Mapping

Integrating climate data with remote sensing for land use prediction.

Disaster Assessment System

Real-time damage assessment for floods, earthquakes, and wildfires.

Ongoing Projects

  1. Automated Real-Time Change Detection in Urban Areas

    Developing deep learning algorithms to automatically detect urban development, building construction, and infrastructure changes using time-series Sentinel-2 and Sentinel-1 SAR imagery. The project aims to enable city planners and environmental agencies to monitor rapid urban growth in near real-time with high accuracy and minimal false positives.
